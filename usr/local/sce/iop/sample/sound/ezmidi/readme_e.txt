EzMIDI - Playing Music and Producing Sound Effects with MIDI

<Description of the Sample>
	This sample allows to play music and produce sound effects 
	by using csl_hsyn, csl_midi, and csl_msin.

	This sample is associated with the sample of the EE and is 
	functional as a set.  For details, refer to the readme of the EE
	(ee/sample/sound/ezmidi/readme_e.txt).
	
<File>
	midi_ent.c
	midi_com.c
	midi_mu.c
	ezmidi_i.h
